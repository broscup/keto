Quick Snack Ideas

  • Cheese sticks, cubes, or slices (Cheddar, Mozzarella, Provolone, etc.)
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Pork rinds (plain) - good for dipping
  • Guacamole (serve with pork rinds or celery)
  • Sour cream dip (mix sour cream + ranch seasoning or spices) - serve with pork rinds or veggies
  • Pepperoni slices (eat alone or with cheese)
  • Deli meat slices (turkey, ham - check labels for sugar)
  • Celery sticks (plain or with cream cheese/nut butter)
  • Cucumber slices (plain or with salt/dip)
  • Olives (green or black)
  • Nuts (in moderation): Macadamia nuts, Pecans, Walnuts, Almonds
  • Seeds (in moderation): Chia seeds, Flax seeds, Hemp seeds, Pumpkin seeds (Pepitas)
  • Pickles (ensure they are sugar-free)
  • Avocado slices with salt or Everything Bagel seasoning
  • Ham/Turkey + cream cheese + pickle roll-ups
  • Deviled Eggs: Mash hard-boiled egg yolks with keto mayo, mustard, salt, pepper, paprika. Refill egg whites.

Treats & Fat Bomb Recipes

  • Peanut Butter Chocolate Fat Bombs: Gently melt 1/2 cup coconut oil and 1/2 cup natural peanut/almond butter over low heat or microwave. Stir until smooth. Remove from heat. Stir in 2-3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder and keto sweetener (like liquid stevia or powdered erythritol) to taste. Pour into silicone candy molds or mini muffin liners. Freeze until solid (about 30-60 mins). Store in freezer.
  • Vanilla Coconut Almond Fat Bombs: Gently melt 1/2 cup coconut oil and 1/4 cup natural almond butter. Stir until smooth. Remove from heat. Stir in 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut, 1 tsp vanilla extract, pinch of salt, and keto sweetener to taste. Pour into molds. Freeze until solid. Store in freezer.
  • Lemon Coconut Fat Bombs: Gently melt 1/2 cup coconut oil. Stir in 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut, 1 tsp lemon extract (or zest of 1 lemon), pinch of salt, and keto sweetener to taste. Pour into molds. Freeze until solid. Store in freezer.
  • Chia Seed Pudding: In a jar or container, combine 2 tbsp chia seeds, 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk (or coconut milk), and keto sweetener to taste. Add 1 tsp cocoa powder for chocolate flavor, or 1/2 tsp vanilla extract for vanilla. Stir or shake well. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, until thick like pudding. Stir again before eating.
  • Coconut Bites: Mix 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut with 1/4 cup melted coconut oil and keto sweetener to taste. Add vanilla or cocoa if desired. Roll into small balls. Refrigerate or freeze until firm.
  • Low-Carb Chocolate: Brands like Lily's (sweetened with stevia/erythritol) or ChocZero (sweetened with monk fruit) offer bars, chips, and sometimes peanut butter cups. Enjoy in moderation.
  • Berries (Small Amounts): A small handful of raspberries, blackberries, or strawberries with unsweetened whipped heavy cream can be an occasional treat (track carbs).
