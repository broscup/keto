Burger Bowls

Recipe/Components:

Cook 1 lb ground beef patties or crumbles. Assemble bowls: bed of lettuce or spinach, cooked beef, crumbled bacon, sliced avocado, pickles, chopped tomato (in moderation), shredded cheddar cheese. Drizzle with sugar-free dressing (like ranch or a mayo/mustard mix).

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce/Spinach
  • Avocado
  • Pickles (sugar-free)
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • Sugar-free dressing (or mayo/mustard)
  • Salt, Pepper

Bunless Burgers + Sautéed Mushrooms & Asparagus

Recipe (Burgers):

Form ground beef into patties, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder. Cook patties (grill/pan-fry) to desired doneness. Top with cheese if desired.

Recipe (Veggies):

Slice mushrooms, sauté in butter with minced garlic, salt, pepper until browned. Trim asparagus, toss with olive oil, salt, pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 10-15 mins or pan-fry until tender-crisp.

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef
  • Mushrooms
  • Asparagus
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder
  • Cheese slices (optional)

Keto Chili

Recipe (Simple):

Brown 1-1.5 lbs ground beef with 1/2 chopped onion and 1 chopped green bell pepper. Drain fat. Stir in 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes (undrained), 2 tbsp tomato paste, 2-3 tbsp chili powder, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper to taste. Add 1/2 cup water or beef broth. Simmer covered for at least 30 minutes, longer for better flavor.

Serving:

Top with shredded cheddar, sour cream, sliced avocado, or jalapeños.

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Chili powder, Cumin, Garlic powder, Salt, Pepper
  • Water / Beef broth
  • Toppings: Cheddar cheese, Sour cream, Avocado, Jalapeños

Keto Meatza (Meat Crust Pizza)

Recipe (Crust):

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). In a bowl, mix 1 lb ground beef (or Italian sausage) with 1 egg, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper. Press mixture thinly (about 1/4 inch) onto a parchment-lined baking sheet into a round pizza shape. Bake for 15-20 mins until cooked through. Carefully drain off any excess fat.

Assembly:

Spread a thin layer of low-sugar pizza sauce over the meat crust. Top generously with shredded mozzarella cheese and desired keto toppings (pepperoni, cooked sausage, mushrooms, olives, green peppers, onion slices). Bake for another 10-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and melted.

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Egg
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Italian seasoning, Garlic powder, Salt, Pepper
  • Low-sugar pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Keto toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, peppers, onion etc.)

Keto Nachos (Pork Rind Base)

Recipe:

Brown 1/2 lb ground beef with taco seasoning (low-carb). Spread pork rinds (or cheese chips*) on an oven-safe plate or baking sheet. Top with cooked taco meat, shredded cheddar/Mexican cheese blend, sliced jalapeños. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 5-10 mins until cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve immediately topped with sour cream, guacamole, and/or low-carb salsa.

*Cheese Chips: Place small mounds of shredded cheddar on parchment paper, bake at 375°F (190°C) until melted and slightly browned/crispy (watch carefully).

Shopping List Items:

  • Pork rinds (plain) or Cheddar cheese (for chips)
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning (low-carb)
  • Cheddar / Mexican cheese blend (shredded)
  • Jalapeños (fresh or pickled)
  • Toppings: Sour cream, Guacamole (avocado, lime, salt, onion/cilantro), Low-carb salsa

Keto Shepherd’s Pie (Cauliflower Mash Topping)

Recipe (Filling):

Brown 1 lb ground beef or lamb with 1/2 chopped onion, 1 chopped celery stalk, 2 minced garlic cloves. Drain fat. Stir in 1/2 cup beef broth, 1 tbsp tomato paste, 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, salt, pepper, herbs (e.g., 1/2 tsp dried rosemary/thyme). Simmer 10 mins. Stir in 1/2 cup chopped green beans or other low-carb veg instead of peas/carrots.

Recipe (Topping):

Steam 1 large head cauliflower until very tender. Drain EXTREMELY well (pat dry). Mash or blend with 2-4 tbsp butter, 2-4 tbsp heavy cream or cream cheese, salt, pepper, garlic powder until smooth.

Assembly:

Spread meat mixture in baking dish, top evenly with cauliflower mash. Optional: sprinkle Parmesan on top. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 mins until bubbly and top is slightly browned.

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Cauliflower
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Beef broth
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Green beans (or other low-carb veg)
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream or Cream cheese
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, Dried rosemary/thyme (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional topping)

Keto Sloppy Joes (Lettuce Wraps/Chaffles)

Recipe:

Brown 1 lb ground beef, drain fat. Add 1/4 cup chopped onion, 1/4 cup chopped green pepper, sauté until soft. Stir in 1/2 cup sugar-free tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce, 1 tbsp yellow mustard, 1 tsp chili powder, salt, pepper, and keto-sweetener to taste (optional). Simmer 10-15 mins. Serve in lettuce wraps, over chaffles, or plain. Serve with side salad (romaine, cucumber, oil/vinegar).

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Green pepper
  • Sugar-free tomato sauce/crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Yellow mustard
  • Chili powder
  • Keto sweetener (optional)
  • Lettuce (for wraps/salad)
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil, Vinegar
  • Salt, Pepper

Stuffed Bell Peppers (Beef & Cauli Rice)

Recipe:

Cut 3-4 bell peppers (any color) in half lengthwise, remove seeds. Pre-bake shells slightly if preferred (10 mins at 375°F). Brown 1 lb ground beef with 1/2 chopped onion. Drain fat. Mix beef with 1 cup riced cauliflower (raw or lightly steamed), 1/2 cup low-sugar marinara or tomato sauce, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, salt, pepper. Fill pepper halves with mixture. Top with shredded cheddar or mozzarella cheese. Place in a baking dish with about 1/2 inch water or broth in the bottom. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 mins until peppers are tender and filling is hot.

Shopping List Items:

  • Bell peppers
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Riced cauliflower (fresh or frozen)
  • Low-sugar marinara/tomato sauce
  • Italian seasoning, Salt, Pepper
  • Cheddar or Mozzarella cheese (shredded)

Taco Skillet

Recipe:

Brown 1 lb ground beef with 1/2 chopped onion. Drain fat. Stir in 1 packet taco seasoning (low-carb) or homemade (chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper). Add 1/2 cup water/broth and 1/4 cup salsa (optional). Simmer 5 mins. Top with shredded cheese, avocado slices, sour cream, jalapeños.

  • Water/Beef broth
  • Salsa (low-sugar, optional)
  • Cheddar/Mexican cheese (shredded)
  • Avocado
  • Sour cream
  • Jalapeños (optional)
  • Salt, Pepper

Zucchini Lasagna

Recipe:

Brown 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage. Stir in 1 cup low-sugar marinara sauce. Slice 2-3 medium zucchini lengthwise thinly (use a mandoline if possible). Pat slices dry. Mix 1 cup ricotta cheese with 1 egg and 1/4 cup Parmesan, salt, pepper. Layer in a baking dish: thin layer of sauce, zucchini slices, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers. End with sauce and mozzarella. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 mins until bubbly and zucchini is tender. Let rest 10 mins before slicing.

Shopping List Items:

  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Zucchini
  • Low-sugar marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Salt, Pepper

Buffalo Chicken Bake (or Dip)

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Mix 2 cups cooked shredded chicken, 4 oz softened cream cheese, 1/4 cup buffalo sauce (like Frank's), 1/4 cup ranch dressing (keto-friendly), 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese. Spread in a small baking dish. Top with another 1/4 cup cheddar. Bake 15-20 minutes until bubbly.

Serving:

Eat as is, or serve with celery sticks or pork rinds. Can also be served over roasted cauliflower.

Shopping List Items:

  • Cooked shredded chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Buffalo sauce (low-carb)
  • Keto Ranch dressing
  • Cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • Optional serving: Celery, Pork rinds, Cauliflower

Buffalo Wings + Ranch + Celery/Cucumber Sticks

Recipe (Wings):

Pat chicken wings dry. Toss with 1-2 tbsp baking powder (NOT soda) and salt for crispiness. Arrange on wire rack over baking sheet. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 40-50 mins, flipping halfway, until crispy and cooked through. Toss hot wings in sauce.

Recipe (Sauce):

Melt 1/4 cup butter and whisk in 1/4 to 1/2 cup Frank's RedHot sauce (or similar low-carb buffalo sauce).

Serving:

Serve with celery/cucumber sticks and keto-friendly ranch or blue cheese dressing.

Shopping List Items:

  • Chicken wings
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Frank's RedHot sauce (or similar)
  • Celery / Cucumber
  • Keto Ranch or Blue Cheese dressing

Chicken Thighs + Brussels Sprouts with Bacon

Recipe:

Cook 4-6 bacon slices until crisp; remove bacon, reserve 1-2 tbsp drippings in pan (or use olive oil). Season 4-6 chicken thighs (bone-in or boneless) with salt, pepper, garlic powder. Sear thighs in bacon drippings/oil until browned, then finish in oven at 400°F (200°C) until cooked through (165°F internal temp). While chicken cooks, trim and halve 1 lb Brussels sprouts. Toss sprouts with olive oil (or reserved drippings), salt, pepper. Roast on a baking sheet at 400°F (200°C) for 20-30 mins until tender and caramelized, shaking pan halfway. Crumble cooked bacon over sprouts before serving alongside chicken.

Shopping List Items:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Brussels sprouts
  • Bacon
  • Olive oil (if needed)
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder

Garlic Butter Chicken Thighs + Roasted Green Beans

Recipe:

Toss 4-6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 35-45 mins until cooked through. Toss green beans with olive oil, salt, pepper; add to the pan for the last 15-20 mins. In the final 5 mins, add 2-3 tbsp butter and 2-3 minced garlic cloves to the pan juices, spoon over chicken/beans.

Shopping List Items:

  • Chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • Green beans
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder

Garlic Parmesan Chicken Tenders + Keto Coleslaw

Recipe (Tenders):

Cut chicken breast into strips. Prepare dredge: mix grated Parmesan cheese, almond flour (optional, 1-2 tbsp for texture), garlic powder, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning. Dip chicken strips in beaten egg, then press into dredge mixture to coat. Pan-fry in olive/avocado oil over medium heat or bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 mins until golden and cooked through.

Recipe (Coleslaw):

Combine shredded cabbage (and a tiny amount of shredded carrot if desired, watch carbs) with keto mayo, apple cider vinegar, keto sweetener (like erythritol/stevia) to taste, salt, and pepper.

Keto Pizza (Fathead Crust - Pepperoni)

Recipe (Fathead Crust):

Microwave 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella + 2 oz cream cheese for 60-90 secs until melted. Stir in 3/4 cup almond flour, 1 egg, pinch of salt. Knead well. Press dough thinly between parchment paper onto a pizza pan. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for 8-10 mins. Add toppings (sugar-free sauce, cheese, pepperoni) and bake another 5-10 mins.

Recipe (Caesar Salad):

Combine Romaine lettuce, grated Parmesan cheese, and sugar-free Caesar dressing. No croutons.

Shopping List Items:

Pork Chops + Cabbage Stir Fry

Recipe (Pork Chops):

Recipe (Stir Fry):

Sausage & Cabbage Skillet

Recipe:

Slice 1 lb pre-cooked sausage (like kielbasa, andouille, or smoked sausage - check labels for low sugar/carbs) into rounds. Heat 1 tbsp olive or avocado oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add sliced sausage and brown lightly (3-5 mins). Add 1/2 chopped onion (optional) and 1 small head shredded cabbage (about 6-8 cups). Cook, stirring occasionally, for 10-15 minutes until cabbage wilts and becomes tender. Season with salt, pepper, maybe 1 tsp paprika or a splash of apple cider vinegar for tang.

Shopping List Items:

  • Pre-cooked Sausage (low-sugar/carb)
  • Cabbage
  • Olive oil / Avocado oil
  • Salt, Pepper
  • Optional: Onion, Paprika, Apple cider vinegar

Creamy Tuscan Salmon + Zucchini Noodles

Recipe:

Pan-sear 2 salmon fillets. Remove. Sauté 2 cloves minced garlic in 1 tbsp butter/olive oil. Add 1/2 cup heavy cream, 1/4 cup chicken broth, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan, 1 cup spinach, 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes (oil-packed, drained). Simmer until sauce thickens slightly and spinach wilts. Return salmon to pan to coat. Serve over zucchini noodles (spiralized zucchini, lightly sautéed or raw).

Shopping List Items:

  • Salmon fillets
  • Zucchini
  • Garlic
  • Butter / Olive oil
  • Heavy cream
  • Chicken broth (or water)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Spinach
  • Sun-dried tomatoes (oil-packed)
  • Salt, Pepper

One-Pan Garlic Lemon Salmon + Spinach

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place 2-4 salmon fillets on the sheet. Drizzle generously with olive oil. Top with 2-4 cloves minced garlic, juice of 1/2 lemon, salt, and pepper. Roast for 12-15 minutes, depending on thickness. In the last 5 minutes of cooking, toss 4-5 cups fresh spinach with a little olive oil, salt, pepper and add it to the baking sheet around the salmon to wilt. Serve immediately.

Shopping List Items:

  • Salmon fillets
  • Fresh spinach
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, Pepper

Shrimp Zoodle Stir Fry

Recipe:

Heat avocado oil or sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté 1 tbsp minced garlic and 1 tsp minced ginger until fragrant (30 secs). Add 1 lb peeled shrimp, cook until pink (2-3 mins). Add spiralized zucchini noodles (2-3 medium zucchini) and other low-carb veggies (e.g., sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets). Stir fry briefly (2-4 mins) until zoodles are slightly tender but not mushy. Toss with 2-3 tbsp soy sauce/tamari, maybe a pinch of keto sweetener or a dash of rice vinegar (optional). Garnish with sesame seeds if desired.

Shopping List Items:

  • Shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • Zucchini
  • Garlic
  • Ginger (fresh)
  • Avocado oil / Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce / Tamari
  • Optional: Bell peppers, broccoli, keto sweetener, rice vinegar, sesame seeds

Broccoli Egg Bake Squares

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish. Whisk 6-8 eggs with 1/4 cup heavy cream or unsweetened almond milk, salt, pepper, garlic powder. Stir in 1 cup steamed chopped broccoli and 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese. Pour into dish. Bake 20-25 minutes until set. Cool slightly and cut into squares.

Serving:

Serve warm or cold, perhaps with cucumber slices or avocado.

Shopping List Items:

  • Broccoli (fresh or frozen, steam first)
  • Heavy cream or Unsweetened almond milk
  • Cheddar cheese (shredded)

  • Optional serving: Cucumber, Avocado
