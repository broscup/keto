Best Choices (Drink Freely)

  • Water (still or sparkling/seltzer - check labels for no additives)
  • Plain Herbal Tea (unsweetened - check ingredients)
  • Black Coffee (unsweetened)
  • Water infused with lemon, lime, cucumber, or mint leaves

Acceptable in Moderation (Check Labels & Track)

  • Unsweetened Almond Milk (check for carrageenan if sensitive)
  • Unsweetened Coconut Milk (carton for drinking, canned for cooking - check for additives)
  • Diet Sodas (sweetened with stevia, erythritol, monk fruit preferred over aspartame/sucralose if possible - use sparingly)
  • Broth (Beef, Chicken, Bone Broth - check labels carefully for added sugars, MSG, or carbs)
  • Heavy Cream or Half-and-Half (small amounts in coffee/tea - track carbs/calories)

Alcohol (Strict Moderation - Limit 1 Serving & Infrequent)

Alcohol metabolism can pause fat burning and significantly impact triglycerides, especially sugary drinks. If consuming, choose very carefully and infrequently.

  • Best Bets (Lowest Carb/Sugar):
    • Spirits (Vodka, Gin, Tequila (silver), Rum (unflavored), Whiskey/Scotch) - Neat, on the rocks, OR mixed ONLY with:
      • Soda Water / Club Soda / Sparkling Water
      • Plain Water
      • A squeeze of fresh lime or lemon
  • Okay Bets (Dry Wines - Approx 3-5g net carbs per 5oz glass):
    • Dry Red Wine (Pinot Noir, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Syrah)
    • Dry White Wine (Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay (unoaked usually lower carb))
    • Dry Ros√© Wine
    • Brut Champagne / Sparkling Wine
  • Generally AVOID:
    • Beer (Regular, Light, and even most Ultra-Light beers have too many carbs)
    • Sweet Wines (Dessert wines, Moscato, Port, Sherry (except very dry), Riesling (unless specified dry))
    • Liqueurs (Amaretto, Kahlua, Baileys, Triple Sec, Schnapps, etc. - very high in sugar)
    • Sugary Mixers (Juice, Tonic Water (regular), Regular Soda, Grenadine, Syrups, Sweet & Sour Mix)
    • Pre-mixed Cocktails / Wine Coolers / Hard Seltzers with added sugar (read labels!)
    • Flavored Spirits (Vanilla vodka, Coconut rum, etc. - often contain hidden sugars)
