Keto Tips & Information

General Keto Tips:

  • Focus on whole foods: meats, leafy greens, and healthy fats.
  • Stay hydrated and replenish electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium).
  • Track your carb intake — stay below 20–50g net carbs daily depending on your goals.
  • Don’t fear fats. Incorporate healthy fats like olive oil, avocado, butter, and coconut oil.

Drink Guide:

  • Water (still or sparkling)
  • Black coffee or coffee with heavy cream
  • Unsweetened tea
  • Diet sodas and flavored waters without carbs (use sparingly)
